Rejuvenate Professional Wood Floor Restorer

Transform your wood floors with a powerful, long-lasting solution that brings back their original beauty and shine. Perfect for both home and commercial use, this formula is designed to restore scratches, enhance color, and deliver a high-gloss finish that lasts for years.

Key Features

  • Restores wood floors with a high gloss finish
  • Durable protection against stains and spills
  • Easy pour-on and mop application
  • No waxy build-up for a natural shine
  • Suitable for both residential and commercial use

Why Choose Us

Whether you're maintaining your floors regularly or revitalizing worn surfaces, Rejuvenate Professional Floor Restorer offers a reliable and effective way to keep your wood floors looking their best for longer. One 32oz bottle covers up to 525 square feet, making it efficient for large spaces and ideal for daily use.

Perfect for Every Surface

This product works seamlessly on all types of hardwood floors, giving them a deep clean and a long-term protective layer. Simply apply with our microfiber mop bonnet for an even, streak-free finish that dries in just 45 minutes. Rejuvenate is the go-to solution for anyone wanting to preserve the elegance and durability of their wood floors.

Flitz Multi-Purpose Metal Polish

A powerful, safe, and versatile solution for all your metal cleaning needs. Whether you're looking to restore old silverware, shine up chrome fixtures, or rejuvenate brass hardware, Flitz is the trusted choice for a brilliant finish that lasts.

Flitz Multi-Purpose Metal Polish is crafted in the U.S. and upgraded with advanced German technology to ensure top-tier performance. It's engineered to clean and polish a wide range of metals without damaging the surface, making it perfect for both delicate and heavy-duty tasks.

  • Non-abrasive formula for gentle yet effective cleaning
  • Works on Aluminum, Chrome, Brass, Nickel, and Silver
  • Removes tarnish, rust, oxidation, and grime efficiently
  • Long-lasting protection in freshwater and saltwater

Versatile and Reliable

Whether you're in your kitchen, bathroom, or working on automotive or outdoor fixtures, Flitz is your all-in-one metal cleaner.
